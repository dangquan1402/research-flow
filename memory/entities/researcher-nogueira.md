## Profile
- **Name:** Rodrigo Nogueira
- **Affiliation:** University of Waterloo (as of 2021)
- **Key Paper:** "Investigating the Limitations of Transformers with Simple Arithmetic Tasks" (2021)

## Contributions
- Early pioneer in studying how number surface form affects arithmetic learning in transformers.
- Demonstrated that subword tokenization fails on 5-digit addition.
- Introduced position tokens (e.g., `3 10e1 2`) that enabled addition up to 60 digits.
- Work predates and directly inspired subsequent research on index hints, Abacus Embeddings, and Position Coupling.
