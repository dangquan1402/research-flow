## Overview

Creator of nanoGPT — the minimalist GPT implementation used as the base architecture in "Teaching Arithmetic to Small Transformers" and many other small transformer studies. Former director of AI at Tesla, founding member of OpenAI.

## Relevance

- **nanoGPT:** The reference implementation for small transformer experiments. Decoder-only architecture with configurable layers, heads, and embedding dimension.
- **nanochat:** Extension of nanoGPT demonstrating consumer-GPU training ($100, 4 hours)
- **transformer_sizing.ipynb:** Notebook in nanoGPT repo for sizing transformer architectures
- **Code:** https://github.com/karpathy/nanoGPT
